## 3) Dr. HatsRx Quiz (MVP)

### Tooling
Use Wix Forms, Typeform, Jotform, Interact, involve.me, or a Wix quiz app.  
**Do not overbuild AI at launch.**

### Quiz Headline
Let Dr. HatsRx Prescribe Your Next Hat

### Quiz Intro
Answer a few quick questions and we’ll match a hat to your mood, energy, and style.

### Questions + Routing Logic
1. **How are you feeling today?**
   - Bold → Bold Energy Rx / Confidence Rx
   - Focused → Focus Rx
   - Creative → Creative Flow Rx
   - Chill → Chill Rx
   - Mysterious → Stealth Mode Rx
   - Rebellious → Bold Energy Rx

2. **How do you want to show up?**
   - Confident → Confidence Rx
   - Clean → Focus Rx
   - Artistic → Creative Flow Rx
   - Low-key → Stealth Mode Rx
   - Loud → Bold Energy Rx
   - Luxury → Confidence Rx / Stealth Mode Rx
   - Street → Bold Energy Rx / Creative Flow Rx

3. **What fit do you prefer?**
   - Snapback
   - Dad hat
   - Trucker
   - Fitted
   - Bucket hat
   - Surprise me

4. **What color energy are you into?**
   - Black / neutral
   - Bright / loud
   - Earth tones
   - Cool tones
   - Monochrome
   - Surprise me

5. **Where are you wearing it?**
   - Everyday
   - Night out
   - Work / grind mode
   - Travel
   - Content creation
   - Event / statement look

> Only include hat fits you actually sell.

### Result Page Template
**Your Prescription Is Ready**

- **Diagnosis:** You’re looking for bold, confident, high-presence energy.
- **Prescribed Collection:** Confidence Rx
- **Recommended for:** stepping out, being seen, making an entrance, moving with purpose.
- **Dosage:** Wear whenever your confidence needs to be seen before you say a word.
- **Possible side effects:** compliments, double takes, elevated posture.

**CTAs**

## 14) Amazon Strategy
Do **not** start on Amazon.

Recommended sequence:
1. Validate winners on HatsRx.com (first 60–90 days)
2. Hold inventory only for proven winners
3. Send winners to Amazon FBA
4. Use Amazon for volume + Prime convenience
