# Acceptance Criteria

## Site

- Homepage loads correctly
- Navigation works
- Collections are clickable

---

## Products

- Each product has:
  - tags
  - description
  - images
  - price

---

## Quiz

- Quiz loads
- Each answer routes correctly
- Result links to correct collection

---

## Checkout

- Test order completes
- Confirmation email is sent

---

## Content

- No placeholder text visible
- Branding is consistent

---

## Rule

Do not launch until all above are working.
